
## 1 Definition 

Prompt Engineering is the practice of designing prompts to guide AI model to generate accurate outputs.

If you only input write me an article about Climate Change, Large Language Model doesn't know your audience, model doesn't know long the article should be and model doesn't know the tone it should maintain. However, if you put: 

- role to model: you are an expert of researching the climate change in EU 
- a context: Germany has experienced an unprecedented surge in temperatures and extreme heatwave.
- a task: Write a comprehensive analysis dealing how carbon dioxide emissions drive climate change within Germany 
- add constraints: 
(1) Length: strictly around 1k
(2) tone: Professional, authoritative, academic
(3) formatting: clear heading, introduction and a conclusion
